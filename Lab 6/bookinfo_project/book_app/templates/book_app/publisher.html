Publisher Information

Publisher: Bloomsbury (UK), Scholastic (US), Raincoast (Canada)

First Published: 8 July 2000 (UK), 1 September 2000 (US)

ISBN: 978-0439139601

Country: United Kingdom

Overview: The book was first published by Bloomsbury in the UK, followed by Scholastic in the US. It was a major turning point in the *Harry Potter* series, with darker themes and a more intricate plot. The book received critical acclaim and won several awards, including the Hugo Award for Best Novel in 2001.

Notable Editions:

  • UK First Edition (Hardcover) - Bloomsbury
  • US First Edition (Hardcover) - Scholastic
  • Illustrated Edition - Released in 2019
  • 20th Anniversary Edition - Released in 2020
